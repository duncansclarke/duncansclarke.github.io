Duncan Clarke

YouTuber and writer, currently living in Toronto, Canada.

I'm known for making videos about the humanities through meme formats such as tierlists and icebergs. I also make video essays.

I am a founding member of the Toronto-based internet culture collective The CCCRU (The Canadian Cybernetics Culture Research Unit), a studio half-jokingly named after the cultural theory group from the 90s. You can read about us here, here and here.

My main interests include cognitive science, psychology, literature, linguistics, and philosophy (particularly philosophy of mind).
